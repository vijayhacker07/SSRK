Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from our users

  1. How to change an incorrectly entered phone number or email?

    Simply delete the monitoring session with the wrong data and run the software again.

  2. How many accounts can I monitor at once?

    The software can monitor up to five different accounts in a single profile at the same time.

  3. What if the account limit is reached?

    If you want to track more than five accounts, you must sign up for an additional account in the Dashboard.

  4. During the registration process the phone number/username/email were provided incorrectly, how can I change it?

    To change your account information, you need to go to the Dashboard Settings.

  5. The Accounts section shows one or more accounts available, what to do next?

    After successfully launching one or more monitoring sessions, you need to replenish the balance by one of the supported methods and access the intercepted archive with the history of the account.

  6. User's photo is not displayed or is not displayed correctly, what does it mean?

    No photo of the user may mean that his account does not have the specified image or that his image is not saved in the account history.
